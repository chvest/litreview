What is a pilot review?

Before starting the full review, Kitchenham recommends that all reviewers independently screen a small shared sample (typically 5–15%) to calibrate their interpretation of the criteria. After the pilot you compare decisions using Cohen's κ and discuss discrepancies. If agreement is acceptable you advance to full review (pilot decisions count); otherwise refine the criteria, reset the pilot, and try again.
